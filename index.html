Transportation

We are providing bus transfer from Reykjavik to Hotel Budir on Friday, August 19th. Please let us know if you are interested in a ride (and tour).

For airport bus transfer you can book a “Flybus” here: http://bit.ly/1KnB2Vx. The price is 2.800 ISK (~$22 USD) per person one way and 5.000 ISK (~$44 USD) roundtrip.

If you prefer a taxi, the price is 16.500 ISK (~$133) for up to 4 passengers + luggage.

For car rentals, you can book your car directly here: www.icerentalcars.is using the discount code purplePink. Be sure to read up on driving safely in Iceland.

We've chosen the amazing Hotel Budir as the homebase for all our wedding events. This is where the chartered bus will pick-up and drop-off our guests. A set of rooms have been reserved for our guests.

The rooms at Hotel Budir come in various sizes and with different amentities, so please make a request if you have specific needs. Breakfast is included with the rooms. Descriptions and prices can be found here. Links to book rooms can be found after RSVPing.

August is the high season in Iceland, therefore availability will be limited everywhere, but if you'd like to stay at a nearby hotel or airbnb, remember that you will need your own car to get to the hotel and to the wedding ceremony. There is no taxi service in remote Iceland.

FAQ

  • Why Iceland? It's one of the first places outside of New York that Liz and Eli visited together. When thinking about the best places in the world to get married, Iceland was a natural choice.
  • What is Iceland's weather like? August is the best time to visit the island, but be prepared for random showers and a sometimes chilly breeze. If you go on a glacier tour, brace yourself for a touch of winter, but with a light water resistant jacket and a little layering, you should be fine.
  • I am planning to spend more time in Iceland, what should I do? We're working with wedding planners Pink Iceland. If you plan on extending your stay in Iceland, please get in touch — birna@pinkiceland.is — about all your travel and accommodation needs. Just mention "Liz & Eli."
