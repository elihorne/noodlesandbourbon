Our Story

In a city of millions, Liz and Eli met at a friend's birthday party (Thanks, Julia!). Liz was leaving for a 3-month adventure around Europe and Eli was dreaming about an "Around the World" plane ticket with his brother. Although they didn't meet again for 2 years, they left lasting impressions on each other.

During an intense winter blizzard, Liz and Eli found themselves alone in New York without friends or family. They went to see a movie, spent the entire night talking about life and travel, and something beautiful - their relationship - began to take form.

Fast forward to today: they travel the world together, and continue to fall in love with each other.

On August 20th, they invite you to celebrate their wedding in one of the first places they traveled to together - Iceland.

Getting There Accomodations The Wedding
The closest major airport is in Reykjavik. If you arrive before Friday, August 19th, we recommend staying at the Hotel 101 and exploring the capital.

Friday, August 19th

On Friday morning, please plan to be at Hotel 101 at 10am to board the bus which will take everyone out to the wedding location.

Upon arrival at Hotel Budir, there will be a cocktail hour, and then you have the rest of the day to get settled.

If you are driving yourself, here is a map with the hotel location.

We've purchased all the rooms at the Hotel Budir for Friday (August 19th) and Saturday (August 20). This is where events like the cocktail hour and reception will take place.

The rooms come in various sizes and with different amentities, so please make a request if you have specific needs. The room descriptions can be found here

After the wedding, there will be the reception back at Hotel Budir

On Saturday morning, the bus will pick up all guests and drive to the wedding location. There will be some small sightseeing stops along the way.

The ceremony will take place at 1pm.

Although August is prime weather time in Iceland, it can still be slightly chilly. Please wear a light jacket in case the winds pick up.
