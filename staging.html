In a city of millions, Liz and Eli met at a friend's birthday party (Thanks, Julia!). Liz was leaving for a 3-month adventure around Europe and Eli was dreaming about an "Around the World" plane ticket with his brother. Although they didn't meet again for 2 years, they left lasting impressions on each other.

During an intense winter blizzard, Liz and Eli found themselves alone in New York without friends or family. They went to see a movie, spent the entire night talking about life and travel, and something beautiful - their relationship - began to take form.

Fast forward to today: they travel the world and continue to fall in love with each other.

On August 20th, they invite you to celebrate their wedding in one of the first places they traveled to together - Iceland.

If you arrive before the wedding, or are staying after, our wedding planner can provide hotel recommendations and activities. Contact Birna - birna@pinkiceland.is and mention "Liz and Eli's Wedding."

If you are renting a car, be sure to read up on driving safely in Iceland.

We've chosen the amazing Hotel Budir as the homebase for all our wedding events. This is where the chartered bus will pick-up and drop-off our guests. A set of rooms have been reserved for our guests.

The rooms at Hotel Budir come in various sizes and with different amentities, so please make a request if you have specific needs. Breakfast is included with the rooms. Descriptions and prices can be found here.

August is the high season in Iceland, therefore availability will be limited everywhere, but if you'd like to stay at a nearby hotel or airbnb, remember that you will need your own car to get to the hotel and to the wedding ceremony. There is no taxi service in remote Iceland.

Reserve a room

We trust everyone will be stylish, but aim for "semiformal/dressy casual" - leave your jeans and sneakers behind. Similarly, no ballgowns required.

Our advice pertains mostly to the environment. There will be a fair amount of being out in nature, so dress accordingly. Bring a jacket and avoid sinking heels.

The average daytime temperature around the coast during August is 10-12°C (50-55°F). A warm summer day would have temperatures around 20°C (68°F).

The reception will be back at the hotel, so you will have a chance to change or slip on those dancing shoes.
