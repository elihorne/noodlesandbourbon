Our Story

Liz and Eli met at a friend's birthday party (Thanks, Julia!). They talked for hours, but didn't meet again for 2 years.

During a winter blizzard, Liz and Eli found themselves at a movie together, spent the entire night talking, and this time something beautiful - their relationship - began to bloom.

Fast forward to today: they are traveling the world, and continue to fall in love with each other.

They invite you to celebrate their wedding in one of the first places they visited to together - Iceland.
